My name is Andrea Farkas.

I was born and raised in Hungary. After a few years of travelling, I met my husband and we moved to Canada to start our family.

We were blessed with two beautiful children who I love with all my heart. I bought my first camera and it became my passion to try and capture every fleeting moment of this wonderful time and make them timeless.

I started this company to try and help others cherish their memories the same way I did.
